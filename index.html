Our Services

Vicio Code Tech has specialize in the following services

Enterprise Software Development

These services typically include tools for managing business processes, data, and resources, such as customer relationship management (CRM), enterprise resource planning (ERP), and human resources management (HRM). They are typically tailored to meet the unique needs of each organization, and are often deployed across multiple departments or business units to support collaboration and efficiency.

API's & System Integration

System integration involves connecting disparate systems or applications to share data and functionality, typically using APIs to facilitate communication between them. APIs are crucial for enabling seamless system integration and can help organizations streamline their workflows, increase efficiency, and improve overall productivity.

Intranet Portals Development

Intranet portals development involves creating a private internal network that provides employees with access to company resources, tools, and information. These portals can be customized to meet the unique needs of the organization, and can include features such as document management, employee directories, and project management tools. Intranet portals can improve communication and collaboration within the organization, as well as enhance productivity and efficiency.

Mobile Games Development

Mobile game development services involve creating games specifically designed for mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets. These services include game design, programming, and testing, as well as distribution and marketing. Mobile game development is a rapidly growing industry and can provide companies with an opportunity to reach a large and engaged audience.

USSD & AI Chatbots

USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) and AI chatbot services are two methods of providing interactive mobile communication services. USSD is a simple menu-based system that allows users to interact with mobile applications using short codes, while AI chatbots use natural language processing to understand and respond to user queries. These services can be used for a wide range of applications, including mobile banking, customer service, and information retrieval.

Consumer Software Develoment

Consumer software development services involve creating software applications specifically for individual users or small businesses. These services can include the development of mobile apps, desktop applications, and web applications, as well as user experience design, testing, and maintenance. Consumer software development can provide businesses with an opportunity to reach a wide audience and can be tailored to meet the unique needs of individual users.

Android & IOS Develoment

Android and iOS development services involve creating mobile applications specifically designed for the Android and iOS operating systems. These services typically include app design, programming, testing, and deployment to the respective app stores. Android and iOS development can provide businesses with an opportunity to reach a large and engaged audience on the two most popular mobile platforms.

Data Management

Data management services involve the organization, storage, retrieval, and protection of data, including databases, data warehousing, and data analysis. These services can help businesses to manage large volumes of data, improve data quality and accuracy, and comply with data security and privacy regulations. Effective data management can provide businesses with valuable insights and enable better decision-making.

Data Analysis

Data analysis services involve the use of statistical and computational methods to extract insights from data. These services can include data visualization, predictive analytics, and machine learning. Data analysis can help businesses to make informed decisions, improve operations, and identify new opportunities for growth.

All Social Media Integration

Social media integration services involve the incorporation of social media functionality into an organization's website or application. These services can include social login, social sharing, and social commenting, among others. Social media integration can help businesses to increase engagement, reach a wider audience, and improve brand recognition and loyalty.

Payment Services

Payment processing services involve the management and facilitation of electronic transactions between buyers and sellers. These services can include payment gateway integration, fraud prevention, and chargeback management, among others. Payment processing services can help businesses to increase revenue, improve cash flow, and provide a seamless customer experience.

Cloud Computing

Cloud computing services involve the delivery of on-demand computing resources, such as servers, storage, and applications, over the internet. These services can include infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and software as a service (SaaS). Cloud computing can help businesses to reduce IT costs, increase scalability and flexibility, and improve accessibility and collaboration.

Bulk SMS

Bulk SMS services involve sending large volumes of text messages to a group of recipients simultaneously. These services can be used for marketing, communication, and customer engagement purposes, and typically involve the use of a web-based platform or API. Bulk SMS services can help businesses to reach a large audience quickly and cost-effectively.

Website Develoment

Website development services involve creating and maintaining websites, including website design, programming, testing, and maintenance. These services can include the development of static websites, dynamic websites, and e-commerce websites, among others. Effective website development can help businesses to establish a strong online presence, attract and engage customers, and increase revenue.

Web Hosting

Web hosting services involve the storage and maintenance of website files on a server, allowing websites to be accessed via the internet. These services can include domain registration, website backup, and website security. Web hosting can help businesses to ensure that their website is always available, secure, and able to handle high volumes of traffic.

Support & Maintenance

System support and maintenance services involve the ongoing management and upkeep of an organization's IT infrastructure, including hardware, software, and networks. These services can include system monitoring, troubleshooting, and software updates, among others. Effective system support and maintenance can help businesses to ensure that their IT systems are running smoothly and efficiently, minimizing downtime and optimizing performance.

IT Consulting

IT consulting services involve providing expert advice and guidance to businesses on how to use technology to achieve their goals and overcome challenges. These services can include strategic planning, system analysis and design, and project management. IT consulting can help businesses to identify and implement technology solutions that improve efficiency, reduce costs, and increase competitiveness.

Value Added Services

System, feature value-added services involve enhancing existing IT systems with additional features and functionality, to provide greater value to businesses and users. These services can include the integration of third-party software, customization of existing software, and the development of new features and modules. Value-added services can help businesses to optimize their IT systems, improve user experience, and gain a competitive edge.
